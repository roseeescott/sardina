We offer unforgettable surf adventures against the rugged backdrop of the Pacific Coast of Nicaragua.

We’re a family run local surf movement based in Popoyo, Nicaragua. Whether you choose us to get you started with surf, help you improve your skills or show you the best surf spots, you can be guaranteed an authentic experience.

Offers

From absolute beginners to experienced surfers, we have the instructors and guides with the knowledge needed to take you to your next level. We want to get you in the water and on the waves as soon as possible but safety comes first. All initial sessions start with a land based briefing which includes a run through of our safety practices.

Surf Lessons

Ideal if you’ve never surfed before, want a bit of a refresher or to pick up some tips on the local breaks. In a group lesson, we ensure a minimum of 1 instructor to 3 students, with in-the-water support at all times. Lessons typically last 2 hours and prices include board hire.

Private lessons from $35 | Group lessons from $30

Surf Packages

If you’re looking to book in for a few lessons or have a goal in mind then a package might be the best option for you.

Practical Information

Sardina is based at Camping Luna, which is in the middle of the best surf breaks and where you can enjoy a night under the stars in one of the hamacas for as little as $5 per night (tents and pitches also available). See our Local Guide section for more details on the surf and other places to stay.

The Weather

The tropical climate brings a wet season which typically lasts from May to December and a dry season from January to April, when you might want to bring a spring suit. With the exception of October (which is usually filled with heavy rain) we really do have world class waves year round.

Nicaragua, the land of the offshore.

Getting Here

To get to the area you’ve got a few options - drive (best in 4x4 or on a decent motorbike), taxi transfer or the local bus which stops in Las Salinas, the local village. Some people even choose to cycle.

Getting to Sardina? We will come to you. Providing you’re staying in the Popoyo area then we will pick you up and drop you back at the end of your adventure with us. If you want to stay with us at Camping Luna, let us know in advance then we can help with directions, arrange a taxi transfer or pick you up from the bus stop in Las Salinas.
